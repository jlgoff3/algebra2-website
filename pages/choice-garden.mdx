# The Garden of Choice

Have you ever wandered through your Algebra 2 class, seen all the empty choice boards, and thought, "What should I put there?" Then the Garden of Choice is your friend! Here at the Garden of Choice, we've collected and pruned some of the finest choice boards for your enjoyment - not for you to take from here, but to give you a glimpse of what your own choice board garden might look like.
